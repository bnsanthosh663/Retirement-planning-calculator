# Retirement-planning-calculator
The Retirment Planning Calculator is a web-based tool that helps users estimate their future financial needs and plan their savings accordingly. It allows users to input their current expenses, expected inflation rate, years to retirement, and lifestyle preferences to calculate the projected cost of living in the future. The application uses financial formulas to estimate future expenses by considering inflation growth over time. It also allows users to adjust their lifestyle multiplier, helping them understand how different living standards may affect their retirement planning. This project is designed to make financial planning simple, interactive, and accessible for individuals who want to better understand their long-term financial goals.
